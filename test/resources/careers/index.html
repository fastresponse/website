Career Services Department

We're here for you!

The Fast Response team is here to help all of our students succeed in every way possible. We will guide you through your educational goals and help prepare you for your future career. Our Career Services Department assists graduates with career planning and job placement. It is very important that you begin planning for your career early! Students will be much more successful at finding an entry-level position with the help of our Career Services team. We will guide you through the job searching process and provide assistance the whole way through.

Fast Response Career Services include:

  • Resume and Cover Letter writing assistance
  • Job search tools
  • Interview coaching
  • Setting up job interviews
  • Listings of available career opportunities
  • Tips on how to dress for success and be professional

Here's how to utilize our Career Services:

  1. Email a copy of your current resume and cover letter to career.services@fastresponse.org.
  2. Review our sample resume and cover letter offerings.
  3. Our dedicated staff will help you to revise your resume and practice your interview skills.
  4. You will be provided with job searching tips, including our most recent listing of available jobs for your field.
  5. One-on-one appointments are available. Contact us using the information below.
