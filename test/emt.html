Emergency Medical Technician

Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs) are trained medical workers who respond by ambulance to medical emergencies for the injured and ill. By the end of class, students will be confident in critically assessing, evaluating, and treating medical and trauma patients.

The EMT-Basic Course at Fast Response is geared to help students succeed and pass the national examination that is required for state certification, the NREMT (National Registry of Emergency Medical Technicians).
