Community CPR

 

This course is appropriate for everyone in the community. A great class for families, grandparents, babysitters, schools, offices, parents, a friend, or anyone wishing to be prepared and help out in the community. 

Covered in this class are adult, child and infant CPR, choking rescues, demonstration of AED (Automatic External Defibrillator) and use of barrier devices. This is an American Safety and Health Institute course. 

  • 5 Hours.
  • AHA Certification good for 2 years
  • EMSA approved Pediatric CPR for Child Care Providers included
  • Not valid for healthcare professionals.
    (See Healthcare CPR for appropriate certification)
