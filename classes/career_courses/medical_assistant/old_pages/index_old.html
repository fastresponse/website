Clinical Medical Assistant

  

Clinical Medical Assistants are multi-component healthcare professionals that provide support to a physician, podiatrist, chiropractor or other health practitioner. Medical Assistants enjoy exciting and multifaceted careers as duties vary greatly and can include taking and recording vital signs and medical histories, rooming patients and preparing them for exams, administering injections, drawing blood and performing commonly ordered lab tests.

Other schools only include venipuncture with their Medical Assistant Course, which is not enough training to draw blood in a hospital environment or work independently in a lab. However, Fast Response includes a complete PHLEBOTOMY CERTIFICATION with our program!

Our 5 month course (most of the time only 3 hours per day!) will prepare you for clinical procedures such as sterile techniques, assisting with minor procedures, injections, phlebotomy and and electrocardiograms (ECG/EKG). A full month of field experience gives you confidence, polished skills and contacts to get your career started right.

According to the US Department of Labor, Medical Assisting is one of the fastest growing and most in-demand professions in the country!

  • AHA CPR Certification and AHSI First Aid Certification 
  • Job Search Assistance 
  • Loans and payment plans to help make tuition affordable
