Phlebotomy Technician (CPT-1)

The Certified Phlebotomy Technician 1 program is designed for anyone wanting to obtain CPT-1 certification in phlebotomy. If you need to change careers, are just starting out, or want to increase your employability in the medical field, this course is ideal.

Included in the program:

  • Dynamic, relevant classroom training
  • Small classes, with a maximum 8:1 ratio for skills instruction!
  • 100 hour program including 40 hours of Clinical Externship
  • Career assistance upon graduation
  • Ongoing refresher courses
  • Flexible payment options
