Sterile Processing Technician

 

Sterile Processing Technicians provide a critical service in the healthcare environment. These technicians focus on infection control and aseptic techniques to disinfect the wide variety of surgical instruments and tools required for surgical procedures. 

This course will introduce the student to the roles and responsibilities of Sterile Processing Technicians. The student will obtain basic knowledge and broad perspective of Sterile Processing. 

Sterile Processing Technician is a 200 hour program with 120 hours of classroom didactics and skills lab practice and 80 hours of externship. 

Classes are held four days a week (Monday, Wednesday Thursday, and Saturday)--Three classes will be 4 hours (Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday) and an 8 hour class on Saturdays. The course lasts 6 weeks.

 

 

Included in the program:

  • Monday, Wednesday, Thursday 6pm-10pm and Saturday 9am-6pm
  • Be prepared for the CBSPD exam
  • Career assistance upon graduation
  • Flexible payment options
