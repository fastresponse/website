Emergency Medical Technician

Accelerated EMT Program

The EMT course at Fast Response is designed for anyone wanting to get into emergency medicine as an EMT. EMTs may work in an ambulance, with a fire department, in a hospital emergency room or on a search and rescue team.

We believe that our five weeks of classroom training with additional field experience provide the most effective and expedient platform for our graduates to continue on to success in the EMT field. Throughout the course, students practice skills in the classroom and the Fast Response Ambulance on-site.

Realistic scenarios and guaranteed externships in both the ambulance and Emergency Room environment give graduates confidence in their abilities.

Included in the program:

  • Dynamic, relevant classroom training
  • Small class sizes with multiple skills instructors
  • Preparation for the National Registry Examination (NREMT)
  • 18 hours of Clinical Externship with both Ambulance and Emergency Room experience guaranteed
  • Career assistance upon graduation
  • Flexible payment options
  • Flexible payment options available
  • Job placement assistance
  • New course every month
  • Clinical externships guaranteed
  • Start a new career path!
  • Affiliated with California Dept. of Rehabilitation and Workforce Investment Act eligible provider
