 Advanced CPT-1 Certification

 

This 20-hour accelerated course is for those already practicing phlebotomy who need to obtain their CPT-1 under the new California State standards. 

You must have documented, and signed by an M.D., at least 1040 hours of on-the-job phlebotomy work experience. 

 

Prerequisites for the Advanced Phlebotomy 
Recertification Program:

 

  • 1040 hours of phlebotomy experience in California within the past 5 years, documented by an M.D. 
  • Experience must include at least 50 venipunctures and 10 skin punctures
  • High School Diploma, GED or college coursework
