ECG Recognition 

12 Lead ECG:

Our advanced ECG course will teach you how to quickly and easily recognize significant cardiac events such as S-T segment Elevation Myocardial Infarction (STEMI).  Also covered in this course: identification of artifact, proper 12-lead electrode placement, and acquisition techniques. The ECG Basic Arrhythmia course or equivalent is a prerequisite to this course.  Includes: One 6-hour day of instruction Fast Response Course Guide (provided on first day of class)

  • One day - 6 hours long
  • Provides 6 CEUs
  • Refresher or Intro course
