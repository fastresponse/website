EMT Refresher

24 Hour EMT Refresher Course

This course is available for EMT-1 license holders in need of continuing education for State recertification. Currently licensed EMTs and those with licenses that are less than 6 months expired are eligible for State recertification upon completion of this course. For licenses expired within the past 12 months, an additional 12 hours of continuing education will be necessary. Our course combines lecture and skills components with the latest equipment available in the EMT field. Whether you are an ER Tech, in Paramedic school, or an EMT, this course will refresh your skills and provide some new information. Our instructors are all experienced EMTs and Paramedics.

Included in the program:

  • 3 days, 8 hours a day.
  • Provides 24 CEUs.
  • Skills Check-Off Included.
