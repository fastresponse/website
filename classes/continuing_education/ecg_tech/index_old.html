ECG Technician:

Our five day ECG Technician class incorporates the information learned in our two day Basic ECG and one day 12-lead classes along with a more detailed review of the Anatomy and Physiology of the heart, electrical systems and cardiovascular systems, along with covering basic Medical Terminology. After successful completion of our five day course and passing the final exam you will be qualified to take the National Center for Competency Testing (NCCT) exam to become a Nationally Certified ECG Technician.

 

  • Medical Terminology
  • Anatomy and Physiology of the Cardiovascular System
  • Acquiring a 3-Lead, 5-Lead, and 12-Lead ECG
  • Rhythm Recognition and Interpretation
  • Excellent Hands on Experience
  • Taught by Registered Nurses, Paramedics, and EMTs with extensive field experience
  • Graduates will be eligible to take the National Center for Competency Testing (NCCT) ECG Exam
  • Fun and interactive learning environment with a strong emphasis on rhythm recognition
