ACLS Preparation Course

This course is designed to prepare students for the fast paced AHA ACLS Provider and Renewal courses.

ECG rhythm recognition and emergency cardiac pharmacology are covered in depth.

In addition, an overview of the ACLS algorithms will be provided.

  • For new students, or students with expired ACLS cards.
  • 7 CEUs are issued to the student upon completion of this class.
  • One Day, 9:00am-5:00pm.

If this is your first time taking ACLS, or if you simply need to brush up on your skills, we HIGHLY recommend this course for you!
