ITLS Advanced Provider Course 

 

Medical Professionals facing patients with immediate life-threatoning emergencies requires advanced knowledge and hands-on skills to take better care of trauma patients. ITLS stresses rapid assessment, appropriate intervention and identification of immediate life threats. The ITLS framework for rapid, appropriate and effective trauma care is a global standard that works in any situation.

ITLS courses combine didactic presentations and realistic hands-on skills stations. Fast Response challenges the student with exciting scenario assessment stations where learning is put to work in simulated trauma situations. Our ITLS course is designed, managed and delivered by course directors, coordinators and instructors experienced in EMS, prehospital care and the ITLS approach.

Our ITLS course provides hands-on practice for: Patient assessment and management, basic and advanced airway management, needle chest decompression and fluid resuscitation, spinal motion restriction -- (rapid extrication, short back board, helmet managment, log roll, and long back board/ scoop stretcher utilization), extremity immobilization and traction splint application


Become the best prepared Emergency Professional you can be by gaining certification in International Trauma Life Support.


ITLS eTrauma Completer Course

  • Interactive didactic presentation in an online format
  • One day, 8:30am-5:00pm, on-campus section in addition to the online section
  • You will receive 8 CEUs from ITLS after completion of the online portion and 8 CEUs from Fast Response following successful completion of skills and the written exam.
