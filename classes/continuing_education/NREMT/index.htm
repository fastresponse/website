NREMT

 

EMT State/National Skills Testing: NREMT Psychomotor Skills Verification

The NREMT course at Fast Response is designed for current EMT Basics needing National Registry Skills Verification for NREMT recertification.  This course meets the State of California Skills Testing Requirements for EMTs to recertify and the National Registry for EMTs (NREMT) skills requirements. The EMT skills training includes: Patient Assessment for Medical and Trauma, Cardiac Arrest Management AED, Oxygen Administration by Non-rebreather Mask, Bag Valve Mask – Apenic Patient, Bleeding Control – Shock Management, Joint and Long Bone Immobilization, and Spinal Immobilization Seated and Supine. This exam is valid for only one year. 

THIS IS NOT AN EMT REFRESHER COURSE. This class is designed for EMTs who have taken an EMT Refresher course that did not include Skills Verification or those who have been instructed to obtain a current skills verification through NREMT.


Included in this skills verification testing:

  • Patient Assessment – Medical
  • Patient Assessment – Trauma
  • Oxygen Administration by Non-rebreather Mask
  • Bag Valve Mask – Apenic Patient
  • Bleeding Control – Shock Management
  • Cardiac Arrest Management
  • Joint Immobilization
  • Long Bone Immobilization
  • Spinal Immobilization Seated
  • Spinal Immobilization Supine
