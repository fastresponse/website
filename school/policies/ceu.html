CEU Classes Cancellation Policy

We do not issue refunds for course fees. All registrations are final.

If you cancel or do not attend the class you have registered for, you will forfeit your entire course fee. You may reschedule an unattended class for 50% of the tuition fee.

CEU Classes Reschedule Policy

No refunds will be issued. All registrations are final.

You may reschedule your course by calling us at least 2 business days prior to your scheduled course date. There is a $5.00 fee for rescheduling a course prior to 2 days from the start date.

If you reschedule your course fewer than 2 business days prior to the course start date, you will be charged 50% of your course fees.

Up to two reschedule requests will be honored per course.

Our Administrative Offices are closed on weekends and holidays. We do not accept rescheduling requests on weekends or holidays.

We do not accept requests left on the answering machine.

CEU Classes Late Arrival Policy

Our classes start on time. Please plan your trip accordingly and remember to allow time for parking.

If you are late for your scheduled class, you will be not be admitted into class and you must reschedule (reschedule fee applies).
