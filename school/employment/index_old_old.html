Employment Contacts and Resources

For all of the following positions we are looking for highly motivated, easy-going, enthusiastic instructors!



Phlebotomy Instructor - Must have at least 3 years working experience (In the last 5 years) as a Phlebotomist.

PALS Instructor - We're seeking friendly, outgoing RNs, MDs and Paramedics to join our PALS teaching team! We offer competitive wages! Qualifications: current PALS Provider cert., extensive pediatric critical care background/experience in-field or clinical, familiarity with current AHA PALS guidelines. Must be willing to drive to class sites.

NRP Instructor - We are looking for friendly, gregarious RNs to teach our Neonatal Resuscitation Program. You must be an experienced NICU, ED, and ICN RN with extensive Neonate experience.

ACLS Instructor - We're seeking enthusiastic Paramedics and RN's to join our teaching team! We offer competitive wages! Qualifications: current ACLS Provider certification, 1yr.+ ECC experience in-field, or clinical, familiarity with current AHA ACLS guidelines. Must be willing to drive to various class sites.

CPR / First Aid Instructor - We are looking for enthusiastic, easy-going EMTs and EMT students to teach our CPR/AED and First Aid classes. You must be able to work in very diverse environments and be very good communicating with people.


Please email your resume to applications@fastresponse.org or send it by fax to (866) 290-2739.
