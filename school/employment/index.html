Employment Contacts and Resources

For all of the following positions we are looking for highly motivated, easy-going, enthusiastic instructors with a passion for healthcare and an excellent abililty to communicate with a diverse population.


  • Phlebotomy Instructor — Must have at least 3 years working experience (within the last 5 years) as a Phlebotomist.
  • PALS Instructor — We're seeking friendly, outgoing RNs, MDs and Paramedics to join our PALS teaching team! We offer competitive wages! Qualifications: current PALS Provider certification, extensive pediatric critical care field experience or clinical, and familiarity with current AHA PALS guidelines. Must be willing to drive to class sites.
  • NRP Instructor — We are looking for friendly, gregarious RNs to teach our Neonatal Resuscitation Program. You must be an experienced NICU, ED, and ICN RN with extensive Neonate experience.
  • ACLS Instructor — We're seeking enthusiastic Paramedics and RNs to join our teaching team! We offer competitive wages! Qualifications: current ACLS Provider certification, 1 or more years of ECC field experience, or clinical, and familiarity with current AHA ACLS guidelines. Must be willing to drive to various class sites.
  • CPR / First Aid Instructor — We are looking for enthusiastic, easy-going EMTs and EMT students to teach our CPR/AED and First Aid classes. You must be able to work in a very diverse environment and have excellent communication skills.
  • EMT Primary Instructor — We are looking for an enthusiastic, easy-going EMT, Paramedic, RN or Physician to serve as the primary educator for our EMT program. You must be able to work in a very diverse environment and have excellent written and communication skills. All certifications must be current (i.e. EMT or Paramedic, CPR, etc…) and in good standing with the Local or State Licensing Authority. Primary instructors will possess the required documented teaching methodology course (i.e. NAEMSE, Fire Marshal 1a &1b, etc…) as well as have a minimum two (2) years of clinical or academic experience. Prior teaching experience is desirable.
  • EMT Lab Instructor — We are looking for enthusiastic, easy-going EMTs and Paramedics to assist our Primary EMT Instructor in the Lab portion of the EMT program. Lab Instructors will be responsible, under the direction of the primary instructor, to oversee skill instruction and practice. All candidates must be able to work in a very diverse environment and have excellent communication skills.
