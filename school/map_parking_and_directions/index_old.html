Driving Directions:

From I-580: Take I-580 (East or West) to I-80 Sacramento/Berkeley. Exit University Ave. Proceed straight for about 1.4 miles. Turn right onto Martin Luther King Jr Way / Grove Street. Turn left on Allston Way (3 blocks south of University Ave). The school is located at 2075 Allston Way.

From I-880: Take I-880 to I-80 heading toward Sacramento/Berkeley. Exit University Ave. Proceed straight for about 1.4 miles. Turn right onto Martin Luther King Jr Way / Grove Street. Turn left on Allston Way (3 blocks south of University Ave). The school is located at 2075 Allston Way.

From I-80 W: Take I-80 toward I-580 East. Exit University Ave. Exit University Ave. Proceed straight for about 1.4 miles. Turn right onto Martin Luther King Jr Way / Grove Street. Turn left on Allston Way (3 blocks south of University Ave). The School is located at 2075 Allston Way.
