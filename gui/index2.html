Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) Provider

This is a fun, low-stress, 2-day class for expired or first-time ACLS certification. The course includes rhythm recognition review, drug therapies, algorithms and electrical therapy. This class offers a stimulating hands-on approach using a supportive, team environment. Our instructors are friendly, professional and experienced.
