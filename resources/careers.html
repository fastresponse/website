Career Services

Fast Response School of Health Care Education offers assistance with the challenge of finding a job in a competitive economy.

Our Career Services Workshop Is Free *

The Career Services workshop will help you to:

  • Learn how to write or revise a professional resume.
    The first point of contact is so important.
  • Develop your interview skills.
    How to make the best first impression, present yourself positively and communicate effectively.
  • Optimize your job searching communication skills.
    Cover letters, key words, common mistakes, branding, follow up and thank you letters.
  • Engage in practice interviews including questions presented to prospective health care employees.
    Learn what and what not to say.
  • Learn from professional resume samples.
    See what the pros say.
  • Learn about weekly job postings that local major employers offer.
  • Optimize your online job searching strategies.

*Our workshop is free to our graduates and to our current students who have fewer than two weeks until graduation.

Workshop Preparation

  • Wear professional interview attire.
  • Bring two copies of both your resume and cover letter (4 copies total).
  • Come with a positive attitude and the motivation to succeed.

When and Where

  • 1st and 3rd Thursday of every month.
  • 12:30 P.M. - 4:00 P.M. You must arrive on time.
  • Fast Response campus: 2075 Allston Way, Berkeley, CA 94704.

Late arrivals will be turned away and must reschedule.
